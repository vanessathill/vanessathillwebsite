Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • Regular cream 400ml
  • Mascarpone 250g
  • 3 Eggs
  • Caster Sugar 2/3 Cup
  • Savoiardi Biscuits, 1pk is enough
  • Coffee
  • Cocoa Powder to dust
  • Marsala 3/4 Cup
    • Note: For stronger tiramisu use 1 cup of marsala or 1/2 cup of grappa

    Method

    1. Make 3 (metric) cups of Coffee and keep in the fridge to cool.
    2. Using an electric mixer, whip the cream to a regular whipped cream consistency.
    3. Meanwhile, use a hand mixer to whip the egg yolks and caster sugar until thick and almost white.
    4. Gently combine the egg mixture, cream and marscapone.
    5. Use a stand mixer to whip the egg whites.
    6. Gently and slowly combine the cream mixture with the egg whites.
    7. Combine the marsala and coffee in a shallow bowl.
    8. Dip each savoiardi biscuit in the coffee mixture and place in the dish. Separate each layer with a layer of cream. Use the cream sparingly.
    9. Dust with cocoa powder and place in the fridge. The tiramisu should rest in the fridge for at least 2 hours before serving.
